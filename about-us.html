About Bestgate Engineering

Founded in 2013, Bestgate Engineering is a small Maryland-based business located in the Baltimore/Washington Metro area. We provide professional services to the U.S. Intelligence Community, Department of Defense, and their industry partners. Our primary NAICS codes are 541511 and 541412.

Learn about our culture

Mission

Bestgate Engineering is committed to bringing the best, brightest, and most passionate software and systems engineers to solve our customers' most difficult and challenging technical problems.

Vision

Bestgate Engineering will strive to inspire and motivate its employees to be great engineers, great colleagues, and great people. Through such characteristics, our team will be recognized as a talented, reliable partner in the eyes of our clients and teammates.

Values

Employee Success

Our employees come first. Excited, empowered, and motivated employees are our most critical asset. Our business is built around the success of our team, and we will do what is in its best interests, to ensure we position our people to succeed.

Trusted Relationships

We know our strengths, competencies, and limitations. With that, we will always deliver exactly what we promise. Our integrity is what will create meaningful business relationships, attract great employees, and create satisfied customers.

Technical Excellence

IT and engineering are our lifeblood. We realize the need to have strong, deep roots in theoretical computer science and engineering, and also to maintain fluency in current and future industry trends. We pride ourselves on being the best, and have a burning desire to remain that way.

Management Team

Our company leads by example. Literally.

Bestgate's leadership team is composed of experienced, practicing engineers, bringing over 40 years of software and systems engineering and project management to the company. From leading project teams, running task orders and communities of practice, to running a business, we have the technical, programmatic, and business expertise to meet the needs of our customers and partners at every level.
